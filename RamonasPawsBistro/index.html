Located off the historic Santa Fe Plaza, Ramona's offers your canine companion a fine dining experience with a regional influence in a warm sophisticated atmosphere. Since opening in 2000, the restaurant has become a favorite for local mutts and visiting maltipoos alike. Recently, Ramona's Paws Bistro enhanced its already stellar reputation by adding a bone bar and expanding the restaurant to accomodate private play dates.
